🏥 Government Health Schemes

Ayushman Bharat

Free health insurance up to ₹5 lakh for poor and vulnerable families.

Janani Suraksha Yojana

Financial support for safe pregnancy and institutional delivery.

National TB Elimination Program

Free diagnosis and treatment for Tuberculosis.

Mission Indradhanush

Free vaccination for children and pregnant women.

💊 Daily Health Care Tips

  • Drink clean and safe water every day
  • Wash hands before eating and after using toilets
  • Eat green vegetables and seasonal fruits
  • Avoid tobacco, alcohol and junk food
  • Sleep at least 7–8 hours daily

🩺 Regular Health Checkups

Blood Pressure

Blood Sugar

Important for diabetes prevention and early detection.

Mother & Child Care

Regular ANC checkups for pregnant women.

Child Immunization

Ensure all vaccinations are completed on time.
