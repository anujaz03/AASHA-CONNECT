Who Are AASHA Workers?

Aasha (Accredited Social Health Activist) workers are frontline health warriors who serve as the connection between families and the healthcare system. They support pregnant women, newborns, children, and elderly citizens by providing essential health guidance.

How They Help Society

• Maternal & child healthcare
• Vaccination support & tracking
• Nutrition & hygiene awareness
• Home visits and surveys
• Health counselling & emergency linkage
